The Beginning of M.A.S.C.

In March of 2020, the coronoavirus, the virus that causes COVID-19, became widespread across the globe, upending the life of every person on it. It is estimated that approximately 3.2 million worldwide, and 577,000+ people in the United States alone, have died as a result of COVID-19. These statistics are not just numbers, they represent lives forever lost, causing a wave of grief, and creating a ripple effect felt throughout the world. In order to adequately put this into perspective, EVERY aspect of human life has been significantly touched by COVID-19. Millions of workers are now faced with unemployment, or the elimination of their jobs altogether. Weddings, funerals, graduations, sporting events, concerts, and vacations have been cancelled or postponed. Going out to dinner with family and friends hasn't even been an option until recently. Most movie theaters have been closed, and remain that way for the time being. Parents have been challenged to balance personal and professional priorities, while trying to ensure their child receives a quality education online. Both, have been working remotely. No matter where you reside on the globe, you've been affected in a multitude of ways you never thought possible. Although there is light at the end of the tunnel, with regards to vaccination distribution, we are still fighting an invisible enemy. Slowly, but surely, we will open businesses and buildings, but will things ever truly be the same? Socialization with others, as we once knew it, in any capacity, has forever changed. It is the importance of this last statement, that has transformed M.A.S.C. from imagination into becoming a reality.

About Us

We are just like you, and have experienced the same soul-crushing lifestyle changes over the last year. And, just like you, we've been trying to achieve a new sense of normalcy. We also, have family and friends we haven't seen in what feels like an eternity, and are eagerly awaiting the opportunity to finally do so. It is for these reasons, that we have discovered our purpose and desire, to make life as comfortable for others as possible during these difficult times. Although we sell a high volume of additional PPE products, our face masks are an essential must-have in the war against COVID-19. Our unique masks are washable, made of 100% biodegradable materials, and good for the environment. They are for personal use, and many employers are becoming proactive by utilizing them to protect their employees. The masks are especially popular among clinics, hospitals, and healthcare settings, offering them to their medical staff and personnel. They are equipped with the following features:
  • Built-in Fans
  • Air Filter & Purifier
  • Feeding Tube
  • Electric Charger/Battery Operated Options
  • Solar Powered
  • Soft, Breatheable, Comfortable Fabric
  • Neck Lanyards
  • Plastic Faceguard for Extra Protection
  • Available in a Variety of Colors & Patterns

The M.A.S.C. Mission

"To provide mass, accessibility, safety, and cost-effective means to obtain an air-purifying face mask."

What We Do & Why We Do It

We do exactly what our mission statement says we do. We provide safe, cost-effective, air-purifying face masks that are easily accessible to the public. We acknowledge how difficult the last year has been, and recognize that there are opposing views about social distancing, face coverings, and receiving the vaccine. Rather than arguing any of these points, we believe we've come up with a viable solution for ALL people that addresses these issues. With our product, family, friends, co-workers, etc., can reunite again safely, regardless of their current status in relation to the virus. For those of us that have not chosen not to vaccinate, and don't particularly want to wear a cloth face mask, this is a great alternative. You can still be in close proximity to the ones you love, and although the use of some type of face covering is necessary, you will find that M.A.S.C. is both comfortable and easy to use. For those of us who have been fully vaccinated, M.A.S.C. can be worn as an additional layer of precaution and protection.
